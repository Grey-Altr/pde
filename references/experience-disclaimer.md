# Experience Product Type — Regulatory Disclaimer

> [VERIFY WITH LOCAL AUTHORITY] — Regulatory requirements (occupancy limits, egress widths,
> noise ordinances, toilet ratios, staffing ratios) vary significantly by jurisdiction and are
> updated frequently. All numerical values in this section represent industry guidance ranges,
> not applicable regulations. Verify every compliance threshold with your local authority having
> jurisdiction (AHJ), fire marshal, and relevant licensing authority before finalizing.

## Usage

This disclaimer block MUST appear inline in every section of critique and handoff output that
contains regulatory values (egress times, ratios, distances, capacity limits, noise levels).

The `[VERIFY WITH LOCAL AUTHORITY]` tag must appear on the same line as or immediately adjacent
to every specific numerical regulatory value. Do not group disclaimers at the end of a section.

## Prohibited Patterns

- Stating a specific regulatory threshold as fact without the `[VERIFY WITH LOCAL AUTHORITY]` tag
- Using "print-ready" without an adjacent prepress disclaimer (see STATE.md Key Decision)
