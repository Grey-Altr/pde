# Design Brief — Tide

**Product:** Tide — Marine Biology Field Research Platform
**Version:** 1.0
**Type:** SaaS Platform
**Target Users:** Coastal ecologists, marine biologists, field researchers

---

## Product Vision

Tide is a field research data management platform for coastal ecologists. It unifies data collection, species identification, tidal cycle correlation, and reporting into a single platform designed around the rhythms of fieldwork.
